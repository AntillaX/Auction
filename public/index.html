How to Play

The Goal

Be the first player to reach 655 points by winning football player cards at auction.

Setup

Each player starts with $1,000. A deck of 28 football player cards is shuffled and the entire sequence is revealed face-up to everyone before play begins.

How Auctions Work

  1. Cards are auctioned one at a time in the revealed order
  2. Anyone can open bidding at $50 minimum
  3. Bids must be in $10 increments and beat the current bid by at least $10
  4. Each bid resets a 10-second timer
  5. Each player can use More Time once per round to reset the timer
  6. When the timer expires, the highest bidder wins the card and pays their bid
  7. If nobody bids, the card is discarded

Winning

The first player to reach 655 points wins instantly. If the deck runs out, discarded cards are recycled. If two full passes through discards produce no bids, the highest score wins.

Strategy Tips

  • Everyone sees the full card order — plan ahead
  • All budgets and scores are public — watch your opponents
  • Sometimes letting a card go is smarter than overbidding
  • Running out of money means you can't bid at all
