How to Play

Goal

First to 644 points wins.

Setup

Everyone starts with $1,000. 35 player cards are shuffled and the order is shown to all before play.

Auctions

  • Cards sold one at a time, in order
  • Open at $50, raise in $10 steps
  • Each bid resets a 10s timer
  • More Time: one reset per player per round
  • Highest bid when time runs out wins
  • No bids → card discarded

Endgame

Hit 644 and win instantly. If the deck runs out, discards recycle. Two silent passes → highest score wins.

Tips

  • Card order is public — plan ahead
  • Budgets and scores are public — read the room
  • Letting a card go can beat overpaying
  • Broke means no bids
